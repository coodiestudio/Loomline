04 — Interaction System
Analogue
by
design

Tensioned Light Architecture

No App.
No Dimmer.
Only Hands.

The interaction is physical. Deliberate. The filament is repositioned across the steel-pin matrix by hand — altering tension, path, and density. Each configuration produces a distinct luminous environment.

  • 01 Route the filament through the upper matrix to concentrate light overhead — for reading, for focus.
  • 02 Extend lateral loops across the frame's midpoint to diffuse light outward and soften shadow edges.
  • 03 Create dense lower-weave patterns to project geometric shadows at floor level.
  • 04 Leave sections of pin-row empty. Absence of light is part of the composition.

"Light engineering.
Redesign space through physical tension."
